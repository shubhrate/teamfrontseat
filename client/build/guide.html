Frontseat User Guide

Welcome to the Team Frontseat UI!

Your Play ID is associated with whatever play diagram you'd like to view.

Simply enter it on the Home page to open up the diagram associated with your play.

For view-only access, go to User View.

For editing access, go to Admin View and enter the password to gain access to the live editing and tracking for your play diagram.
